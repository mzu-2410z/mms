Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on the scope. A full brand identity package typically takes 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive web development project can range from 8-16 weeks. We prioritize quality and clear communication throughout the process.

How involved do I need to be in the process?

We believe in collaboration. We require your input during the initial strategy, moodboarding, and key revision stages. Beyond that, we handle the heavy lifting to allow you to focus on your business.

Do you offer retainer services?

Yes. For clients requiring ongoing design and editing support, our retainer packages provide dedicated hours each month for prioritized work, maintenance, and strategic further design work.

What platforms do you build websites on?

We are platform-agnostic but specialize in custom WordPress development, headless CMS solutions like Sanity and Strapi, and static site generators like Next.js.

Can you help with branding from scratch?

Absolutely! We love building brands from the ground up. Our process includes market research, logo design, color palettes, typography, and brand guidelines.
